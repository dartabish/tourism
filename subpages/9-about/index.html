About

About the company

Espree Holidays was born out of a passion for discovery and adventure, founded by a seasoned traveler whose life has been a tapestry of unforgettable journeys. Our founder has ventured through the hidden corners of the world, gathering not just memories but a profound understanding of what true exploration entails. With a wealth of personal travel experience, the inception of Espree Holidays was a natural progression - a venture aimed at sharing the joy of travel with others. We're not just a tour and travel company; we're a collective of enthusiasts committed to curating experiences that go beyond the ordinary. At Espree Holidays, we understand that travel is not just about places; it's about new perspectives, cultures, and the bonds formed along the way.

Our values

The core of Espree Holidays is built on a foundation of key values that reflect the essence of travel as we see it: Exploration, Accessibility, and Hospitality.

  • Exploration: We are driven by a relentless curiosity about the world and its diverse cultures, landscapes, and experiences. We encourage our travelers to step off the beaten path and discover the true essence of their destinations.
  • Accessibility: Travel is a right, not a luxury. We are committed to making travel more affordable and accessible to people from all walks of life. We strive to create packages that cater to a wide range of budgets and travel styles, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to explore the world.
  • Hospitality: We believe in the power of hospitality to transform travel experiences. To us, every traveler is a valued guest, and we go the extra mile to ensure they feel welcomed, comfortable, and cared for. Our goal is for every Espree Holidays traveler to feel at home, wherever their journey takes them.

Our mission

At Espree Holidays, our mission is simple yet profound: to make travel affordable and accessible to everyone. We believe that the world is a book, and those who do not travel read only one page. Our aim is to open up the myriad chapters of this vast book to our clients, allowing them to experience the diversity, beauty, and wonder of our planet. We are dedicated to removing barriers to travel, ensuring that everyone can explore the world's wonders regardless of their budget. Our personalized services are designed to make our clients feel at home, no matter how far away from home they are. With Espree Holidays, every journey is an opportunity for growth, learning, and creating lasting memories.
